Project Case Studies

Case Study: Reporting System Automation

Challenge: Leadership lacked timely, high-quality data. The month-end reporting process was a 5-day, manual, and error-prone cycle in Excel.

Approach: I re-engineered the entire data pipeline. I architected a new data model, automated extraction with Python, and built a dynamic, self-service dashboard in Power BI.

Result: Cut reporting cycle by 60% (from 5 to 2 days) and eliminated 80+ hours of manual work per month, enabling faster, data-driven decision-making for the Board.

Case Study: Commercial Cost Optimisation

Challenge: A £160M manufacturer faced margin erosion from volatile costs. The business lacked visibility into its true cost drivers.

Approach: I led a 12-month cross-functional analysis of procurement and logistics. I built new "what-if" models to simulate pricing scenarios and identify cost-saving opportunities.

Result: Presented findings of ~£1.4M in potential annual savings to leadership. My models were adopted to improve forecast accuracy by 18% and create more resilient pricing.
